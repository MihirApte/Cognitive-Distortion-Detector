## Quick Reference - 10 Distortion Types

(Include this as a section description in Google Form)

1. **All-or-nothing thinking** - Seeing things as completely good or completely bad, with no middle ground. "If I'm not perfect, I'm a total failure."
2. **Overgeneralization** - Drawing a big conclusion from one event. "This always happens to me."
3. **Mental filter** - Focusing only on the negatives and ignoring the positives.
4. **Should statements** - Believing there are rigid rules about how people must behave. (Doesn't always use the word "should".)
5. **Labeling** - Reducing yourself or others to a single label. "I am a loser."
6. **Personalization** - Blaming yourself (or someone else) for something that wasn't fully in their control.
7. **Catastrophising/Magnification** - Blowing things out of proportion. "If I fail this, my entire future is ruined."
8. **Emotional Reasoning** - "I feel it, so it must be true." Treating feelings as facts.
9. **Mind Reading** - Assuming you know what someone else is thinking or why they acted a certain way.
10. **Fortune-telling** - Expecting things to go badly without real evidence.
